Karate: Green Belt!

July 27, 2025 · karate journey green belt belt test belt progression

I’ve now reached Green Belt!

What did we learn?

Post on this coming soon

Tournament

In the process of earning this belt I competed in my first tournament. I performed H-Form #2. I also sparred in both regular as well as team sparring.

Team sparring was a lot of fun I definitely recommend it!

Techniques

  • Jump Front Kick / Mae Tobi Geri
  • Spinning Hook Kick / Ushiro Ura Mawashi Geri
  • Crescent Kick / Mikazuki Geri
    • Inside-Out Crescent Kick / Uchi Mikazuki Geri
    • Outside-In Crescent Kick / Soto Mikazuki Geri
  • Spinning Roundhouse
  • Spinning Backfist / Ushiro Uraken Uchi
  • Jab / Kizami-zuki
  • Hook Punch / Kagi-zuki
  • Back Ridge Hand / Ura Haito Uchi

Katas

We continued to work a lot on H-Form #1, H-Form #2 and H-Form #3. These were all on our test.

The new one this time for me was To-San. This was a really fun one to learn!

Next Belt!

Now we are ready to move on to the next belt. Next up Blue!
