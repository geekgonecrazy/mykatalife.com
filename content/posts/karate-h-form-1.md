The **H Form #1** is a foundational kata.  This kata is named "H" due to its pattern resembling the capital letter 'H' when performed, involving a series of blocks and punches executed in a specific sequence.

<!--more-->

## Outline of H Form #1 Steps:

### 1. **Bow In:**
- Begin in the attention stance (**Heisoku-dachi**), perform a formal bow (**Rei**).

### 2. **Attention Stance:**
- Transition to the ready stance (**Hachiji-dachi**), standing with feet shoulder-width apart and arms relaxed at your sides.

### 3. **Look Left:**
- Turn your head to the left, preparing for the upcoming movements.

### 4. **90° Left Down Block:**
- Pivot to the left into a front stance (**Zenkutsu-dachi**) and execute a left downward block (**Gedan-barai**).

### 5. **Lunge Punch:**
- Perform a lunge punch (**Oi-zuki**) with the right hand, stepping forward into the punch.

### 6. **180° Right Down Block:**
- Pivot 180 degrees to the right into a front stance, executing a right downward block.

### 7. **Lunge Punch:**
- Perform a lunge punch with the left hand, stepping forward into the punch.

### 8. **90° Left Down Block:**
- Pivot 90 degrees to the left into a front stance, executing a left downward block.

### 9. **3x Lunge Punches:**
- Perform three consecutive lunge punches with alternating hands, stepping forward each time.

### 10. **270° Left Down Block:**
- Pivot 270 degrees to the left into a front stance, executing a left downward block.

### 11. **Lunge Punch:**
- Perform a lunge punch with the right hand, stepping forward into the punch.

### 12. **180° Right Down Block:**
- Pivot 180 degrees to the right into a front stance, executing a right downward block.

### 13. **Lunge Punch:**
- Perform a lunge punch with the left hand, stepping forward into the punch.

### 14. **90° Left Down Block:**
- Pivot 90 degrees to the left into a front stance, executing a left downward block.

### 15. **3x Lunge Punches:**
- Perform three consecutive lunge punches with alternating hands, stepping forward each time.

### 16. **270° Left Down Block:**
- Pivot 270 degrees to the left into a front stance, executing a left downward block.

### 17. **Lunge Punch:**
- Perform a lunge punch with the right hand, stepping forward into the punch.

### 18. **180° Right Down Block:**
- Pivot 180 degrees to the right into a front stance, executing a right downward block.

### 19. **Lunge Punch:**
- Perform a lunge punch with the left hand, stepping forward into the punch.

### 20. **Both Hands Out:**
- Extend both hands forward, palms facing out, as if pushing an opponent away.

### 21. **90° Left Arms Crossed to Attention Stance:**
- Pivot 90 degrees to the left, crossing your arms in front of your chest, then return to the attention stance.

### 22. **Bow Out:**
- Perform a formal bow, concluding the kata.
