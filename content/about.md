A blog about my karate journey. Notes from class, breakdowns of techniques and stances, terminology I'm trying to remember, and the occasional belt test write-up.

I started as a white belt in college, got my yellow belt, then stepped away for over a decade. My kids picked karate up at our local dojo, I joined to keep them motivated, and the journey resumed where it had left off.

This site is mostly for me — a place to think out loud about what I'm learning. If anything here is useful to you, all the better.
